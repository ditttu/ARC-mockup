UG Academics

This wing seeks the responsibility in academic related work of ARC. It acts as an interface between the Institute Policy makers and the students onmmatters related to academia.

This wing is where you can put forward your viewpoint in bringing up a change in the academic policies and work on them along with improving your communication skills.

The major functions of this wing are:

Gathering of the standpoint of the students on the matter of critical importance.
Unfurling the academic policies, rules and regulations to the campus junta.
Lending hands to the students with their templates, semester and course planning.
Providing help and guidance to the UG students from professors on matters like policy changing, acedemic assistance by acting as the bridge between the two.

This wing plans to improve the communication between the professors and students by providing them with an online platform for open discussions on topic of common interest related to academia and policies. We aim to convice and conduct regular monthly sessions for the students and professors for frequent interactions.
